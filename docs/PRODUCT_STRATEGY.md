# MTimer - Product Strategy & Roadmap

## 1. Product Vision
To be the definitive companion for the Transcendental Meditation (TM) practitioner, combining precision timing with a gamified emotional experience to drive consistency and long-term habit formation.

---

## 2. Market Positioning
- **Focus:** Niche but dedicated TM audience.
- **Differentiator:** The **Companion System**. Unlike sterile meditation apps, MTimer builds an emotional bond where the user's progress directly affects their Companion's well-being.
- **Precision:** Strictly adheres to the MT phases (2-20-3).

---

## 3. Business Model (Monetization)

### Phase 1: Subscription (SaaS) - Priority
- **Model:** Monthly/Annual Premium tier.
- **Premium Features:**
  - Advanced ambient sounds (Rain, Forest, Binaural Beats).
  - Detailed analytics and progress insights.
  - Cloud sync across devices.
  - Special Companion evolution paths.

### Phase 2: Microtransactions (IAP) - Future
- **Skins & Sounds:** One-time purchases for exclusive Companion appearances or curated soundscapes.
- **Boosts:** Non-progression-breaking cosmetic multipliers.

---

## 4. Success Metrics (KPIs) - Internal Testing
For current internal tests and initial rollout:

1. **Stability (Crash-Free Users):** >99.9%. Focus on audio and timer reliability.
2. **Onboarding Conversion:** Percentage of users who complete the 3-step onboarding and start their first session.
3. **Daily Retention (D1/D7/D30):** Percentage of users who meditate at least once a day.
4. **Engagement (Companion Interaction):** Number of times users tap or interact with the Companion.
5. **Session Completion Rate:** % of started sessions that finish the full core + cooldown.

## 6. Target Audience
- **The "Beginner":** Needs guidance, reminders, and the Companion's "encouragement."
- **The "Expert":** Focuses on the precision of the timer and consistent history tracking.
- **The "Tech-Savvy Practitioner":** Values UI/UX and a modern, agile interface.
